Frequently Asked Question (FAQs)

Are there any projects in this course ?

Yes, This course includes multiple AI-focused projects, which will be covered progressively. Detailed information can be found in the syllabus section above.

What if i have a doubt in a particular topic ?

You can join our Discard group from the classroom page by clicking the “Discord” button. Our mentors will be available there to help resolve your doubts. Additionally, while watching the recorded live classes, you can use the ‘Ask Doubt’ button to submit any questions.

Is the course live ?

Yes, this is a live course. All Web Development content will be taught live. You’ll receive Aptitude, Reasoning, and DSA as recorded sessions, so you can learn them alongside or after the daily Web Dev live classes whichever suits you best.

Who can join this course ?

Anyone interested in becoming job-ready! Whether you are a beginner or looking to enhance your skills.

Who are the instructors in this course ?

The course will be taught by Harsh Sharma Sir and top instructors at Ahmad Coding School.

Are there any prerequisites for this course ?

Yes a laptop & internet connection. That all you need to get your dream job.

How many hours per day will the sessions be ?

Each session will last 1.5 hours, but it also depends on the topics being covered. Some topics may require more time, and we also address students doubts and questions during the sessions.

How long will I have access to this course ?

You will have access to the course for a full 1.5 years. During this time, you can revisit the lessons, review the content, and practice at your own pace.

What if I miss a live session ?

Don’t worry! All sessions are recorded and will be available for you to watch anytime. The recording will be available on the next day of class.

What projects will I work on during the course ?

Yes, you’ll receive tasks for each project demonstrated by the instructor to help reinforce your learning.
